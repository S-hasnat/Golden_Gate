About Us

For years, Golden Gate has been serving Flint hearty breakfasts, delicious coney islands, and homestyle dinners.

Our Story

Since our founding, Golden Gate Restaurant has been a cornerstone of the Flint community. We take pride in serving quality, homestyle meals that remind you of grandma's cooking.

Our commitment to fresh ingredients, friendly service, and a welcoming atmosphere has made us a local favorite for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. From our famous breakfast specials to our mouth-watering coney islands, every dish is prepared with care and tradition.

Whether you're joining us for a quick bite or a family dinner, we promise a dining experience that will keep you coming back for more.
